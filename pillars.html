The 7-Layer System

The Defensible Compliance Architecture

The DCA continuously evaluates your enterprise across seven critical, interlocking layers of governance — through which risk must flow completely and without interruption to constitute a defensible compliance program.

The system does not just score the layers individually. It analyzes the connective tissue between them — a risk that is visible but not documented, escalated but not corrected, or corrected but not governed, is as indefensible as a risk that was never seen at all.

Each layer feeds a dedicated axis on the DCAI — producing a single, quantifiable measure of architectural defensibility.

Proprietary Metric

The DCAI Score

The Defensible Compliance Architecture Index distills complex seven-layer architectural evaluation into a single, unified, quantifiable measure of compliance integrity.

The DCAI acts as the ultimate leading indicator for regulatory readiness — allowing boards and leadership to mathematically quantify their defensibility before regulators ever ask a question.

Each axis of the DCAI corresponds to one of the seven layers, enabling rapid identification of which structural dimension requires remediation.
