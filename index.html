The Compliance Paradox

Activity without architectural proof is indefensible.

Compliance programs fail not because activity is absent — but because institutions cannot prove that the activity works under regulatory scrutiny.

TSeven9 shifts the question from "Are we doing compliance?" to "Can we prove, structurally and evidentially, that we are defensible under scrutiny?"
